Angela recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 Egg plus 1 Egg yoke
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3 cups of bread flour

For The Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 table spoon grounded cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup of Butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix the milk with yeast, sugar and Egg
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour untill combined into a Dough
  4. Knead the Dough for 10 mins
  5. Transfer the Dough into a large bowl, and cover with a plastic wrap, leave for 2 hours
  6. After the dough has double in size, Roll it into a large rectangle
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and the cinnamon
